## Plot panel

***Plot panel*** is the individual plot windows in the `Overview` tab.

On the top of the panel, there are some components to decide what to plot:

- ![fluent--database-multiple-20-regular](https://pic-md-1259550128.cos.ap-nanjing.myqcloud.com/fluent--database-multiple-20-regular.svg) is for selecting sample, embedding and information to show (feature or metadata)
- ![fluent--settings-24-regular](https://pic-md-1259550128.cos.ap-nanjing.myqcloud.com/fluent--settings-24-regular.svg) is for panel-specific settings.
- ![iconoir--filter](https://pic-md-1259550128.cos.ap-nanjing.myqcloud.com/iconoir--filter.svg)is for data-filtering by condition.

<img src="https://pic-md-1259550128.cos.ap-nanjing.myqcloud.com/image-20250103102655375.png" alt="image-20250103102655375" style="zoom:50%;" />

Clicking the legend on right could control the cells to be ***highlighted***.
